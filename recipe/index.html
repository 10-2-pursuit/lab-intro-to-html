Mocha Cake Ali G prolli be eatan'

This is a damn cool mocha cake and how to makes it dawg. Check it. Read dem steps y'all. Respek. Here be da recipe bruv.:

    7" Chocolate Cake 4/0.819kg Mocha Naturally Flavored Bettercreme® Allen® Ganache Dark Chocolate Naturally & Artificially Flavored (#11587) Cocoa Powder Paillete Sequins
    DIRECTIONS 1 Use a speed icer to fill and ice the entire cake 2 Use a scraper to smooth the sides and top. 3 Drizzle heated ganache around the top edge of the cake allowing it to drip down, then fill in the top. Smooth the ganache on top if necessary. 4 Once the ganache dries, dust with cocoa powder. 5 Use an open coupler to pipe rosettes on top. 6 Use a #22 tip to pipe stars on top. 7 Garnish the rosettes with Paillete Sequins 8 Use a #10 writing tip to pipe bottom border
