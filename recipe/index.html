How to Make a Bacon Grill Cheese

*Note: The ingredients listed is for one serving, you may double it to fit the number of people you are serving or to your own liking.

Ingredients

  1. 2 slices sourdough bread (or bread of your choosing)
  2. 4 slices bacon
  3. 1 ounce of cheddar cheese
  4. 1 ounce of mozzorella

Directions

  1. Cook bacon in a pan until crisp. After fully cooked, remove bacon from pan and drain.
  2. Spread butter on the outside of each bread slice and put face down in the pan.
  3. Place cheese and bacon over 1 slice of bread in the pan and then place the other slice on top.
  4. Grill sandwich until golden, about 4-5 minutes. Flip and grill the other side until golden.

Outside Opinion (You may stop reading but just hear me out, please)

When it comes to this dish, it can be paired with anything and you can have it at any time. But....I truly believe that this bacon grilled cheese is best paired with an amazing tomato soup topped with some basil, especially on a cold or rainy day.
