The Perfect Pina Colada

Description

This Pina Colada is made from a classic recipe and will keep you cool & satisfied on hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple Juice
  • Coconut Cream
  • Light Rum

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Add crushed ice and blend until it's well combined and slushy.
  3. Pour into glasses and garnish with fresh pineapple.
