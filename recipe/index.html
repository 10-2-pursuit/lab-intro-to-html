Chicken Ceaser Salad

Chicken Ceaser Salad is a classic salad dish that combines fresh lettuce,grilled or roasted chicken,croutons,Parmesan cheese,and a creamy Ceaser dressing. It is a flavorful and satisfying salad option often enjoyed as main course

Ingredients:

  • 2 grilled or roasted chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • Ceaser dressing

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, sliced grilled chicken, Parmesan cheese, and croutons.
  2. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Caesar Chicken Salad!
