The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread using the butter knife.
  2. Spread the jelly on the other side using the same butter knife.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together to form a sandwich.
  4. Use sharp knife cut sandwich in half
